SURVIVAL TIPS & TRICKS

We can't be solving every case everywhere at the same time, so here are some helpful tips to keep yourself safe from the monsters until we get there.

Salt

Salt, most commonly used against ghosts and demons, our most usual case. It's just a good thing to have in general. it can be an extra to your food or add some extra years to your life.

Whenever there's cold spots, the best thing to do is to put a salt circle around the whole family. That'll keep the ghost away. And, if they show up, go throw some salt on them. It'll buy some time. It'll also knowck a ghost right out of the body it's possessing.

Own a rifle? Make some salt rounds to shoot those sons of bitches with. It'll buy more time than just sprinkling on them.

Iron

Iron, another good ghost repellant, just less available than salt. Still, one touch of this and it'll also knock the spirit out of the body it's possessing.

Always have some kind of iron object like an iron bar, iron knucles, just about anything made of pure iron will work.

Iron is also used against other creatures, such as fairies, phoenixes, acheris, and many others. They can also be used to block witches' powers, though you'd need things like iron cuffs.

Silver

Silver, an effective tool in repelling and deflecting supernatural creatures. It has a large scope of creatures that it affects. Just a touch usually already harms a monster. Silver knives are the best weapon to have against those weak to silver.

It'll work against shapeshifters, fairies, revenants, and, of course, werewolves.

Devil's Trap

Afraid of demons? Here's a simple solution: The Devil's Trap.

There are plenty of places you can place these sigils. One good place is under your living room rug. That'll keep your house nice and safe while not conflicting with your design taste. If you're on the go and afraid of attacks, put a devil's trap on the ceiling of your vehicle. That way, the demon will be trapped in your car while you escape. Just hope they don't ruin your car.

You could also carve them on handcuffs, but it's gonna take some good amount of strength and/or cunning to cuff a demon.
