THE SUPERNATURAL BOOKS

The Supernatural Books, written by Carver Edlund, real name Chuck Shurley, depict the travels and tales of the Winchester Brothers. Being in publication for nearly a decade, these books give quite a lot of history and detail into all the various escapades the brothers have had, from all the gore of hunting to the cruise of a drive.
